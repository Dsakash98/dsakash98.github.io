Why Our Work is different?


Most harmful Air Particles for Developing Countries will be detected. As reported about pollution in Dhaka city, Methane gas is one of the main pollutants. We will detect it and incorporate into our model. We consider required bandwidth. Data will be processed in Cloud Server. A light processor will be used in the measurement device. Electrically recycled components will be used for energy efficiency.

  • We are using NodeMCU

    • NodeMCU is a low-cost open source IoT platform. It initially included firmware which runs on the ESP8266 Wi-Fi SoC from Espressif Systems, and hardware which was based on the ESP-12 module. Later, support for the ESP32 32-bit MCU was added.
